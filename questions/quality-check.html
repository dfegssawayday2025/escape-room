Open envelope A

Does the data make sense?

This is about strengthening quality in the specification, upstream checks, data transfer and validation checks, to ensure the data makes sense before we apply any processing or aggregation to it.




✔



Does the process make sense?

This is about strengthening quality in the process we apply to the data, for example through code reviews, checking calculations and aggregations, as well as checking the process produces outputs that are internally consistent and abide by our data standards.




✔



Does the story make sense?

This is about interrogating the result of our data processing to ensure the story it gives us makes sense, for example checking plausibility over time, local variation, impact of changes, coherence with other sources and sector knowledge.
